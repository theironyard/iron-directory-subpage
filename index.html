The Iron Yard Apps

Iron Directory for iOS

An employee directory for phone, email, and social media.

First Time Installation Steps

  1. On your iOS device, tap this link to GET THE LATEST VERSION. There is no confirmation that the app was downloaded, but it will appear on your phone's screen.
  2. If you try to run it the first time, it will say that the developer is not trusted. Go to Settings > General > Device Management > TIY Academy, LLC > Trust
  3. Open the app. It will say that you need to get a token. Select “Get Token” and fill in your information. An e-mail will be sent to your TIY account.
  4. Open the email (on your iOS device) and click the link. It will open the app and you should be good to go.
